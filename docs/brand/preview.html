Kinetic · the connect moment

The brand's single orchestrated moment, same grammar as the app's connect flood (L4): the host node lands, the wire routes across, the client node pops live. Mechanical easing, runs ONCE — the at-rest mark stays static. Standalone asset: logo-signal-s-kinetic.svg (respects prefers-reduced-motion). For launch screens, socials, loading states.

Ship format · macOS 26 Liquid Glass

The dock icon ships as SlopDesk.icon (Icon Composer bundle, this folder): flat slate fill + two glass layers (wire, nodes) so Tahoe renders native specular/shadow around a still-flat mark instead of auto-glassing a single PNG — the artwork itself stays zero-ornament. Node/wire ratio is the trend-corrected 1.2× (r11 on the 96 grid); the squircle in these mocks is the Apple icon-grid superellipse, and the system applies the real mask at render time.

Verdict

A — Signal S is the recommendation: it is the only mark that is simultaneously the monogram, the product story (one wire, two live machines), and a state display (drain the nodes to grey = offline, exactly MERIDIAN L1). It survives 16 px, needs no gradient or ornament, and the endpoint dots rhyme with the connection cluster's health plate already in the app. B is the most “terminal” but muddies at menubar sizes; C is beautiful big and dead small.
