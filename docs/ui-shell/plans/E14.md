**Epic.** Surface OSC 9;4 progress as a wire-carried per-pane spinner/progress badge; complete
notification delivery + policy (OSC 9/777/99 banners, BEL beep, dock bounce, Notify-While-Foreground /
on-Finish/Error/Watch); add dock progress + red-on-error; finish the privilege surface (title-report
toggle, OSC-52 read/write Ask/Allow/Deny, system-permission status row, IPC guards on the ctl socket).

- `touchesFloatCodec: false` — no FEC/codec/controller float math. Progress percent is integer 0–100;
